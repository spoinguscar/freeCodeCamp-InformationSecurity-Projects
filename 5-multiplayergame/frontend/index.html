Catch the Spoingus!!!!

Spoingus is orange and vicious. He is famously known as the "Slippery Bastard".

You will respawn if you bump into another player. You will respawn and lose a point if you try to get out of the grid.

Use the arrow keys to control your block.

Score: 0, Rank: 1

Give a man a spoingus, scar him for one day. Teach a man to catch spoingus, scar him for life.
